Tap the + button on the top right. Then, choose your map image from your photo library. Use the map to align your image, and make sure to set your opacity and map type because they are saved for later. Finally, tap Done and choose a name for your map.

Either hold down on a map or tap the three vertical dots (⋮). This will open up a dialogue where you can share to any service, including: AirDrop, email, and saving it to notes or another file saving service. iMessage currently does not work. Here is a link to the issue.
